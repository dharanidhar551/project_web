    <p class="break-word">One of the Seven Wonders of the World, Taj Mahal is indeed an amazing place which depicts the India culture and eternal love.      

	Taj Mahal is located in the south bank of Yamuna River in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Agra is the city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. The city is famous for being the capital of the Mughal Empire from 1526 to 1658.

	Taj Mahal means “a mosque like building”, built in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The conventional story behind it is Shah Jahan’s eternal love for Mumtaz.

	It is a large, white marble structure standing on a square base and consists of a symmetrical building with an arc shaped doorway topped by a large hemisphere and needle. On the long sides, a massive vaulted archway, frames the Wang, with a similar arch-shaped balcony above.

	Specialty of Taj Mahal is the white domed marble vault at Agra in India, one of the most admired memorials. Today it is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The architecture of Taj Mahal is a combination of Islamic, Persian and Indian style.

Nearest place to the Taj Mahal is Agra Fort. One of the famous tourist places in India.

By Air: The fastest way of reaching Taj Mahal, Agra is by air. The city of Taj, Agra, has its own airport that is around 7 km from the city center. 
By Rail: There is a good network of trains connecting Agra with the rest of the country. Apart from the main railway station of Agra Cantonment, there are other 2 stations also, that of Agra Fort.
By Road: There are regular bus services from Agra to a number of important cities. The main bus stand of Idgah has a number of buses running for Delhi.
